Welcome to Colossal RPG

Colossal RPG is a small buisness based in Portland Oregon. We make everything you need for your table.

We sell a variety of things for both DMs and players such as Paper Miniatures, Spell Cards, and Dice Boxes.

If your intrested in anything please Email us at Colossalrpg@gmail.com!

About Me

I'm a local artist who simply loves playing boardgames, specifically Dungeons and Dragons.I started drawing Miniatures a local table that I run and people began to ask where I had bought them, and just like that Colossal RPG was born. Since then I've branched out into other things that everyone may need for their game.
